#### Meetings

Unless explicitly agreed otherwise, meetings are held **virtually via Microsoft Teams**. 

In-person meetings at my office are also possible by prior arrangement.

---

#### Meeting Requests & Preparation

Please send meeting requests **via email** to: [boehmt@hochschule-trier.de](mailto:boehmt@hochschule-trier.de)

Requests should include:
  + the **topic and goal** of the meeting,
  + the requested **duration**,
  + your **availability over the next seven days**.

For research or thesis-related meetings:
  + send or upload all relevant material (exposé, drafts, slides, data)
  + no later than **18:00 on the evening before the meeting** 
  (file-sharing details will be clarified individually)

Requests that do not meet these criteria may not receive a response.
